About Andy

Hello! I’m Andy Wainer.

Andy Wainer is a recently retired Community College Educator and Employment Counselor, with Master’s degrees in Education and Career Development. As a Teacher and Counselor, Andy has worked with about 10,000 students and clients. He has taught the classes and workshops listed and described on this website since the 1990’s, and continues to do so in retirement. Andy has also specialized in teaching Comedy Improv, having taught more than 1,000 Improv classes, workshops and programs to all ages, but enjoys teaching all of the classes listed. He has been profiled several times in the Rivertowns Enterprise newspaper, and has been interviewed several times on WVOX AM radio, both in Westchester county, NY, where he lives with his wife. Before the pandemic, Andy and his Improv troupe had a monthly show on Greenburgh Public Access TV. Andy is Vice President of the White Plains chapter of Kiwanis International, a global organization dedicated to helping children and families in need.

ANDY WAINER’S INTERACTIVE

PROGRAMS / WORKSHOPS / CLASSES

  • BOOST YOUR MEMORY POWER – Improves Memory skills by learning and practicing Mnemonic devices, techniques and strategies you can apply on a daily basis. Includes Association, Visualization, Acronyms, Acrostics, and more. Easy to learn, fun to use, practical, and will also improve overall “brainpower”! 2 – 6 sessions
  • COMEDY IMPROV – Bond with others by learning, doing and performing Comedy Improv games and techniques. Games and exercises will spark your imagination, creativity and problem solving abilities. You will create on-the-spot actions, sounds, characters and scenes designed to build positive group dynamics and trust, all while having fun. You will also sharpen your communication skills and become a more confident risk-taker. No experience needed! Any number of sessions!
  • THINK POSITIVE: HOW TO ACHIEVE YOUR GOALS - Learn practical, easy-to-use strategies and techniques designed to make you a more positive person with confidence in yourself and your ability to achieve your goals, tackle new endeavors, feel better about yourself and life in general, and engage more with others. 2 – 4 sessions
  • ANDY’S NOODLE EXERCISE – Engage in gentle chair exercises using a foam (swimming) noodle, easy to learn so you can continue on a daily basis on your own. Good, fun, gentle workout for all. Can be done standing as well. Any number of sessions!
  • ANDY’S COMEDY HOUR – Have some laughs listening to Andy tell his selection of tried-and-true traditional jokes. 1 session
  • DROP-IN IMPROV CLASSES - Andy has a weekly drop in improv class in Ardsley. It’s usually from 7 to 9 PM, usually on Tuesday evening, sometimes on a different evening. No prior experience necessary! Contact Andy for more information FIRST CLASS FREE
